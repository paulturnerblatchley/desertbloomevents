NIGHTMARE SCENARIO

October 30, 2025 • Redlands, CA • One Night Only

How do you react when everything goes wrong? Nightmare Scenario invites you to explore the art in the chaos, the divine in the devil, the survivor in the blood bath as the worst that can happen does happen.

Join the Inland Empire's most creative voices to scare, thrill and excite

Submission Requirements

EXT. FILMMAKER'S STUDIO - WITCHING HOUR
A FILMMAKER reviews the sacred rules carved in stone:
• Films must be UNDER 10 MINUTES (including credits)
• No minimum length - terror comes in all sizes
• Created after JANUARY 1, 2024
• English or English subtitled/dubbed
• All entrants must be 18+ years old
• One entry per submission fee
The filmmaker nods, understanding that submission does not guarantee screening. Only the chosen few will compete on Halloween night.
